Dr.Praneeth

MBBS, MS, FICO

Ophthalmologist

Dr.Praneeth, MS, is an experienced, fellowship-trained Specializing in refractive surgery

Dr. Praneeth is one of the most experienced ophthalmologists in Hyderabad, having performed over 30,000 procedures.

Dr. Praneeth has performed thousands of laser vision correction procedures such as LASIK, Cornea, and Oculoplasty. As a highly skilled surgeon, Dr. Praneeth also performs corneal, and scleral tear repairs and has extensive experience with issues related to glaucoma, cataract surgery, and diabetic eye exams. He also performs all general eye evaluations, as well as retinal laser surgeries, and intravitreal injections for diabetic retinopathy and macular problems.

He has successfully completed his masters at Narayana Medical College and completed a Cornea fellowship and FICO from Sankar Foundation eye hospital, the best outgoing fellow in Sankar Foundation Eye Hospital, where he refined his procedural and surgical skills. Dr. Praneeth has received the Prathibha Award in recognition of his outstanding academic achievements.

Dr.Praneeth is one of the best refractive surgeons in Shaikpet, Hyderabad, to use advanced technology and sophisticated equipment for treatments on his patients. Dr. Praneeth has successfully completed several certifications, Internships, and fellowships to master his medical and surgical skills.

Dr. Praneeth credits his impeccable results in refractive surgery to his technique, experience, special care for his patients, and the use of the most advanced FDA-approved technology available.

Dr Praneeth has done his long term Fellowship in cornea, cataract & Refractive surgery from Sankar Foundation Eye Hospital & Institute, Vizag. He has a decade of rich experience in the field of Ophthalmology who combines skill with kindness & compassion.

He is an expert in all kinds of anterior segment surgeries including cataract, refractive surgeries, corneal transplantation & Trauma, repair etc..

He conducts surgeries with utmost care, expeditiously with great results & remains up to date on advancements in the field He has excellent communication skills to encourage patients & create positive environment.
